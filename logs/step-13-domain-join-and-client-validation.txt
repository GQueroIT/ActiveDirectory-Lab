Step 13 - Domain Join and Client Validation

Configured CLIENT01 with a static IP on the internal lab network.
Set CLIENT01 DNS to 192.168.56.10.
Verified connectivity to DC01 with ping and nslookup.
Joined CLIENT01 to relentixtest.local.

Verified CLIENT01 domain membership from System Properties.
Confirmed CLIENT01 appeared in Active Directory.
Moved CLIENT01 into the Workstations OU.
Validated the computer object with PowerShell on DC01.
