# Step 08 - Admin Account and OU Creation

## Objective
Create the primary administrative account and build the first layer of domain organization.

## What I Completed
- Created the gquero administrative account
- Added the account to Domain Admins
- Created the OU structure under the domain
- Built the following OUs:
  - IT
  - HR
  - Workstations
  - Servers
  - Service Accounts

## Outcome
The domain now has a dedicated administrative account and a clean OU structure for future users, systems, and services.
